Welcome to Math Concepts in Computing Ⅱ !

Recitation summaries will be collected on this page as we progress through the semester.

All problems and exercises mentioned below, unless otherwise indicated, are from Kenneth Rosen's textbook, Discrete Mathematics and its Applications, 7th edition, McGraw Hill Inc.

I will sometimes demonstrate ideas using Matlab. You are not required to use Matlab (or any other scientific computing software) in this class. However, if you are already familiar with Matlab, you may find it helpful to take a look at the code I will provide during the semester. This may clarify the material covered in class. Scientific computing software comes in especially handy when we cover topics from linear algebra later in the semester.
